Eco Friendr

We are driven by our “mission” of enabling individuals, communities and businesses to help create a positive impact on the environment by catalysing them to adopt to new processes, technology and products. We work towards bringing together eco-aligned artisans, vendors, manufacturers and services to the forefront thereby assisting in reducing respective carbon footprints

India’s 6th busiest airport, which is situated in the downtown of Hyderabad has set a benchmark for the green buildings in India.Eco-Friendly Projects

The structure of the airport is designed in a way so as to consume less water, electricity and conserves natural resources. Within the campus of the airport, there is a green belt of 273 hectares with numerous plants. In the last couple of years, RGIA has been successful in saving energy for nearly 3.97 million kWh and have reduced the carbon footprint by 3331 tons.
